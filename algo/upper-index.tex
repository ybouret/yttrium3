We have a $n \times n$ matrix, and we want to index the upper diagonal with
 $$i \in [1:n],\; j \in [i:n]$$
leading to $\dfrac{n(n+1)}{2}$ terms indexed with:
$$
	1 \leq k \leq \dfrac{n(n+1)}{2}
$$

Let us take the $i$-th row: its last index is
\begin{equation}
L_i = \left(\sum_{l=1}^i (n+1-l)\right)  = i(n+1) - \dfrac{i(i+1)}{2}
\end{equation}
