Let $\bm{P} \in M_{n,m}$ be a matrix of row vectors $n\leq m$.
Then:
\begin{equation}
	(\vec{x} \in \mathbb{R}^n) \in \mathrm{Im}(\bm{P}) \iff \exists \vec \lambda \in \mathbb{R}^m, \vec{x} = \bm{P}^T \vec \lambda
\end{equation}


\begin{equation}
	\vec{x} = \bm{P}^T \vec \lambda + \left( \vec{x} - \bm{P}^T \vec \lambda \right)
\end{equation}
